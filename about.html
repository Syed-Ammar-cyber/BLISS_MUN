About Bliss MUN

OVERVIEW
✦

Bliss Model United Nations is a premier platform dedicated to nurturing young leaders through an immersive and engaging experience in diplomacy, international relations, and global politics. Founded with the vision of equipping delegates with comprehensive political and geopolitical knowledge, Bliss MUN brings together the best committees, an experienced and dynamic Executive Board panel, and a passionate, dedicated team committed to making this conference both educational and enjoyable. Our MUN promises not only rigorous debate and meaningful collaboration but also a fun and inspiring environment where delegates can develop their skills, build lasting connections, and ignite change. At Bliss MUN, diplomacy meets brilliance, and together we prepare the next generation of informed, responsible global citizens and future leaders.

Date: 23rd - 24th August 2025

FOUNDER'S
MESSAGE

It gives me immense pride to address you as the founder of Bliss Model United Nations. When I established Bliss MUN, my vision was to create a transformative platform where young minds could immerse themselves in the complexities of global affairs. Bliss MUN is more than just a conference—it is a dynamic space that provides educational, political, and geopolitical knowledge to every delegate who participates. Through rigorous debate, negotiation, and collaboration, our delegates gain firsthand experience in diplomacy and international relations, preparing themselves to be thoughtful leaders and informed global citizens. Our mission is to bring together aspiring diplomats from diverse backgrounds, empowering them to analyze pressing world issues, propose innovative solutions, and drive meaningful change. In an era where international cooperation is more crucial than ever, Bliss MUN stands as a testament to the power of dialogue and the belief that the next generation can shape a better, more peaceful world. Thank you for being a part of this journey and for your commitment to learning, understanding, and making a difference. Together, let us continue to inspire change and foster a spirit of global citizenship.

Udit Pratap Singh

Founder | Secretary General
